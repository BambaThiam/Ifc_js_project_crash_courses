Homepage

Why this website ?

This website was created to popularize the openBIM. It provides, thanks to the IFC.JS library, applications on specific themes around the BIM directly accessible through the browser without installing software. This website is a marketplace for developers wishing to sell their talents, for professionals looking for applications specific to their business problems, for all people sensitive to the openBIM. Thanks to the open source library IFC.JS, this site makes the use of BIM simple and accessible directly through your web browser without installing software. We enrich the gallery with simple and useful use cases for the different stakeholders of a construction project. IFC.js hub plateform is a unique place to reveal the BIM level of your project with an approach based on data and 3D visualization. Together, let's make open-BIM great again.

How does it work ?

    You can :
  • - Use the applications for your business issues
  • - Develop BIM applications on subjects that are important to you and and monetize them
  • - Buy applications that you care about to use it or improve it

About us ?


Who am I?


An engineer learning web development, who believes in open-BIM and who thinks it is useful to have a platform to access the various applications that are constantly growing. Indeed, we all need to find ourselves in all these software/tools offers and I think it is important, at the dawn of this revolution of open-BIM with ifc.js, that we have a hub allowing to better popularize the works around ifc.js. This site is a continuation of the remarkable work done by the ifc.js community. I also think that the high costs related to BIM are brakes to its development. However, the stakes of our generation are enormous in the face of challenges related to the environment, safety, quality, social responsibility, etc. Digital in the broadest sense must be accessible.
Together, let's make our world great again.
